## If Telegram user's name is "@Admin" - then username here should be "Admin"
## Available roles:
## - GM(Teleporting/Banning/Kicking players)
## - ADMIN(Same as GM plus: Sending server messages, starting/restarting/stopping server)
##

## Admin=ADMIN
